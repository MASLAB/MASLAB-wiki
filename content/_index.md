# MASLAB
Mobile Autonomous System Laboratory ([MASLAB](https://maslab.mit.edu)) is an IAP robot building competition. In this competition, you and your team will design, prototype, build, and program an autonomous robot from scratch. Your robot will face off in a public competition to complete tasks involve identifying and manipulating objects without any human intervention. The teams with the best robots will take home extravagant prizes! You will develop not only robotic skills but also teamwork and management during this exciting January, so bring your friends and let's bond over building robots!  

![MASLAB team building robot](/images/intro_team.jpeg)

At the end of the competition, you and your team will get to keep the robot and all the provided materials.
